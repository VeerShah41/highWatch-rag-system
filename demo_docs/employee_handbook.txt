HIGHWATCH AI — EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
Version 3.0 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025
================================================================

1. WELCOME TO HIGHWATCH AI
----------------------------
Welcome to the Highwatch AI family. This handbook outlines the policies,
procedures, and expectations for all employees. Please read it carefully
and reach out to HR at hr@highwatch.ai if you have any questions.

2. WORKING HOURS & ATTENDANCE
-------------------------------
- Standard working hours: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Monday to Friday)
- Core hours (must be available): 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM IST
- Employees are expected to log in via the HRMS portal daily
- Absences must be reported to your manager before 9:30 AM
- More than 3 unexcused absences in a month will result in a written warning

3. LEAVE POLICY
----------------
Annual Leave:
- All full-time employees receive 18 days of annual leave per calendar year
- Leave must be applied 3 days in advance via the HRMS portal
- Maximum carry-forward: 5 days (expires March 31 of the following year)
- Leave cannot be taken during project go-live periods without manager approval

Sick Leave:
- 12 days of paid sick leave per calendar year
- Medical certificate required for illness exceeding 2 consecutive days
- Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year

Maternity Leave:
- 26 weeks of paid maternity leave as per Indian law
- Must be applied at least 4 weeks before expected due date
- HR must be notified immediately upon confirmation of pregnancy

Paternity Leave:
- 10 days of paid paternity leave
- Must be taken within 3 months of the child's birth

4. CODE OF CONDUCT
-------------------
All employees are expected to:
- Treat all colleagues with dignity and respect
- Maintain confidentiality of client and company data at all times
- Not engage in any form of harassment, discrimination, or bullying
- Dress professionally for client meetings (formal or smart casual)
- Not engage in outside employment that conflicts with company interests

5. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
-----------------------
- Reviews are conducted bi-annually: June and December
- Ratings: Exceptional / Meets Expectations / Needs Improvement
- Salary revisions are tied to performance ratings
- Employees rated "Needs Improvement" for two consecutive cycles may face
  performance improvement plans (PIP)

6. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
-------------------------
- All business expenses must be pre-approved by your manager
- Submit expense claims within 30 days of incurring the expense
- Receipts are mandatory for all claims above Rs. 200
- Reimbursements are processed in the next payroll cycle
- Travel expenses must follow the travel policy outlined in Section 9

7. REMOTE WORK POLICY
----------------------
- Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval
- Full-time remote work requires VP-level approval
- Remote employees must be reachable during core hours
- Company equipment must be secured and not used for personal projects

8. RESIGNATION AND EXIT
------------------------
- Notice period: 30 days for junior roles, 60 days for senior/manager roles
- Serving notice is mandatory; buyout requires HR and manager approval
- Exit interview is mandatory before final settlement
- All company assets must be returned on or before last working day
- Full and final settlement is processed within 45 days of last working day

9. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
-----------------------
- Raise grievances at grievances@highwatch.ai
- All grievances are reviewed within 5 business days
- Employees can escalate unresolved issues to hr-head@highwatch.ai
- Anonymous reporting available via the internal ethics hotline
