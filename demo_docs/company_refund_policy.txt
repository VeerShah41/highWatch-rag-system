HIGHWATCH AI — COMPANY REFUND AND RETURNS POLICY
Version 2.1 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025
================================================================

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
This document outlines the official refund and return policy for all products
and services offered by Highwatch AI. All customer-facing teams must adhere
to these guidelines when handling refund requests.

2. ELIGIBILITY FOR REFUNDS
---------------------------
Customers are eligible for a full refund under the following conditions:
- The refund request is submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.
- The product or service has not been fully used or consumed.
- A valid proof of purchase (invoice or order ID) is provided.
- The issue is due to a product defect or service failure on our part.

3. NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS
------------------------
The following are NOT eligible for refunds:
- Custom enterprise solutions that have been delivered and accepted.
- Annual subscription plans after 14 days of activation.
- API credits that have been consumed.
- Services rendered by third-party partners.

4. DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE PRODUCTS
----------------------------------
If a customer receives a damaged or defective product:
- They must report the issue within 7 days of receiving the product.
- A photo or video evidence of the damage must be submitted via email to
  support@highwatch.ai with subject line: "Damaged Item - Order #[ORDER_ID]"
- Upon verification, a full replacement or refund will be issued within 5-7
  business days.

5. REFUND PROCESS
------------------
Step 1: Customer submits refund request at support@highwatch.ai
Step 2: Support team acknowledges within 24 hours (business days)
Step 3: Investigation and verification: 2-3 business days
Step 4: Approval/Rejection communicated via email
Step 5: If approved, refund is credited within 5-10 business days

6. PARTIAL REFUNDS
-------------------
Partial refunds may be issued in the following cases:
- Monthly subscription cancelled mid-cycle (prorated refund for unused days)
- Service partially delivered due to technical issues on our end
- Promotional items where only part of the order is returned

7. ESCALATION PROCESS
----------------------
If a customer disputes a refund decision:
- They may escalate to refund-escalation@highwatch.ai
- A senior team member will review within 3 business days
- Final decision is communicated in writing

8. CONTACT INFORMATION
-----------------------
Refund Team Email: support@highwatch.ai
Escalation Email: refund-escalation@highwatch.ai
Working Hours: Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM IST
Response SLA: 24 hours for initial acknowledgement
