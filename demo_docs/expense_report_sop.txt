HIGHWATCH AI — EXPENSE REPORT & REIMBURSEMENT SOP
Version 2.0 | Effective Date: January 2025
================================================================

1. PURPOSE
-----------
This SOP provides step-by-step instructions for employees to submit
business expense claims and for managers to review and approve them.

2. WHAT CAN BE CLAIMED
------------------------
Eligible expenses include:
- Business travel (flights, trains, taxis, local transport)
- Client entertainment (meals, events) — up to Rs. 3,000 per person
- Conference/event registration fees
- Business software or tools (pre-approved only)
- Office supplies purchased for business use
- Internet reimbursement for remote employees: up to Rs. 1,500/month

NOT eligible:
- Personal meals (unless during client entertainment)
- Alcohol (unless explicitly client-approved and budgeted)
- Luxury or business-class travel (unless approved by VP)
- Gym memberships or personal subscriptions
- Traffic fines or parking violations

3. STEP-BY-STEP: HOW TO SUBMIT AN EXPENSE CLAIM
-------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Collect all receipts at the time of purchase.
        - Digital receipts (screenshots, email PDFs) are accepted
        - Receipts must be in the employee's name or the company name

Step 2: Log into the HRMS portal at hrms.highwatch.ai

Step 3: Navigate to Finance → Expense Claims → New Claim

Step 4: Fill in the following fields:
        - Date of expense
        - Expense category (travel, meals, software, etc.)
        - Amount (in INR)
        - Business purpose (brief description)
        - Project/client code if applicable
        - Attach receipt (PDF, JPG, or PNG — max 5MB per file)

Step 5: Submit the claim for manager approval.

Step 6: Manager has 5 business days to approve or reject.
        - Rejected claims must include a reason in writing

Step 7: Approved claims are forwarded to Finance automatically.

Step 8: Reimbursement is processed in the next payroll cycle.
        - Claims submitted before the 20th of the month are processed
          in the same month's payroll
        - Claims after the 20th roll over to the next month

4. TRAVEL EXPENSE GUIDELINES
------------------------------
Domestic Travel:
- Economy class for flights under 3 hours
- Business class requires VP approval
- Hotel: up to Rs. 5,000/night in Tier-1 cities; Rs. 3,500 in Tier-2
- Daily allowance (DA): Rs. 800/day for meals and incidentals
- Book travel through the company travel portal (travel.highwatch.ai)

International Travel:
- Requires director-level approval minimum
- Business class for flights over 8 hours
- Hotel: up to USD 200/night
- Submit claims in foreign currency; Finance will convert at RBI rate

5. ADVANCE REQUESTS
--------------------
- For expenses over Rs. 10,000, you may request an advance
- Submit advance request 5 business days before travel
- Unused advances must be returned within 3 days of return
- Advance settlement form must be submitted with receipts

6. MANAGER APPROVAL GUIDELINES
--------------------------------
Managers must:
- Review all claims for policy compliance
- Approve or reject within 5 business days
- Add comments for any rejected line items
- Escalate unusual claims over Rs. 20,000 to Finance Head

7. CONTACTS
------------
For expense queries: finance@highwatch.ai
HRMS technical issues: it-helpdesk@highwatch.ai
Travel booking: travel@highwatch.ai
