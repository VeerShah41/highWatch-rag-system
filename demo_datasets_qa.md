## Evaluation Questions & Answers

### 1. Company Refund and Returns Policy

*   **Level: Easy**
    *   **Question:** Within how many days of the purchase date must a refund request be submitted?
    *   **Expected Answer:** A refund request must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.

*   **Level: Medium**
    *   **Question:** What is the complete procedure for reporting a defective product?
    *   **Expected Answer:** The customer must report the issue within 7 days of receiving the product. Photo or video evidence must be sent via email to support@highwatch.ai with the subject line: "Damaged Item - Order #[ORDER_ID]". Upon verification, a full replacement or refund is issued within 5-7 business days.

*   **Level: Hard**
    *   **Question:** A customer had their refund request rejected and is very unhappy. Explain the exact steps they need to take to escalate the issue and the expected resolution timeline.
    *   **Expected Answer:** The customer can escalate the dispute by emailing refund-escalation@highwatch.ai. A senior team member will review the case within 3 business days, and the final decision will be communicated to the customer in writing.

### 2. Data Compliance and Privacy Guidelines

*   **Level: Easy**
    *   **Question:** How long is customer account data retained after the account is closed?
    *   **Expected Answer:** Customer account data is retained for 3 years after account closure.

*   **Level: Medium**
    *   **Question:** What are the steps involved in the Data Breach Response Procedure?
    *   **Expected Answer:** The procedure includes 7 steps: (1) Identify and contain the breach immediately, (2) Notify CISO and Legal within 1 hour, (3) Assess the scope of the breach, (4) Notify affected individuals within 72 hours if it is a high-risk breach, (5) Report to regulatory authority within 72 hours, (6) Document the incident in the breach register, (7) Conduct a root cause analysis and implement preventive measures.

*   **Level: Hard**
    *   **Question:** A customer exercises their "right to erasure" and requests the deletion of all their data. However, they made purchases 2 years ago. How should Highwatch AI handle this request according to the policy?
    *   **Expected Answer:** Highwatch AI must delete the customer's account data and usage logs. However, under the Data Retention Policy, transaction records must be retained for 7 years as per financial regulations. So, the customer's transaction data cannot be deleted yet, but the rest of their personal data should be erased where legally permissible.

### 3. Employee Handbook

*   **Level: Easy**
    *   **Question:** What are the core working hours during which employees must be available?
    *   **Expected Answer:** The core working hours are 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM IST.

*   **Level: Medium**
    *   **Question:** Can you explain the Remote Work Policy?
    *   **Expected Answer:** Employees may work remotely for up to 2 days per week with their manager's approval. Full-time remote work requires VP-level approval. Remote employees must be reachable during core hours, and company equipment must be secured and not used for personal projects.

*   **Level: Hard**
    *   **Question:** An employee in a Managerial role resigns and wants to leave in 30 days. What is the policy regarding their notice period and settlement?
    *   **Expected Answer:** The notice period for senior/manager roles is 60 days. Serving the notice is mandatory, and a buyout requires approval from both HR and the manager. The employee must also complete an exit interview and return all company assets on or before their last working day. Full and final settlement will be processed within 45 days of their last working day.

### 4. Expense Report & Reimbursement SOP

*   **Level: Easy**
    *   **Question:** What is the maximum allowable expense for client entertainment per person?
    *   **Expected Answer:** The limit for client entertainment is up to Rs. 3,000 per person.

*   **Level: Medium**
    *   **Question:** How does an employee submit a claim for business software they purchased?
    *   **Expected Answer:** First, the software must be pre-approved. The employee must log into the HRMS portal (hrms.highwatch.ai), navigate to Finance → Expense Claims → New Claim, fill in the date, category, amount, and business purpose, and attach the digital receipt (PDF/JPG/PNG, max 5MB). The claim is then submitted for manager approval.

*   **Level: Hard**
    *   **Question:** An employee needs Rs. 15,000 for an upcoming international business trip. What is the procedure for requesting this advance, and what rules apply after they return?
    *   **Expected Answer:** Because the amount is over Rs. 10,000, they can request an advance. They must submit the advance request 5 business days before travel. Upon return, any unused advance must be returned within 3 days, and an advance settlement form must be submitted along with the receipts for the spent amount.

### 5. IT Security Standard Operating Procedure

*   **Level: Easy**
    *   **Question:** What is the minimum required length for an employee's password?
    *   **Expected Answer:** 12 characters.

*   **Level: Medium**
    *   **Question:** What qualifies as a security incident according to the IT Security SOP?
    *   **Expected Answer:** Security incidents include: received or clicked phishing emails, unauthorized access to systems, data leakage/accidental sharing of confidential info, lost or stolen devices, and malware or ransomware detection.

*   **Level: Hard**
    *   **Question:** A project manager needs to share client financial records with an external vendor. How should this data be classified and what steps must be taken before sharing?
    *   **Expected Answer:** Client financial records are classified as Level 3 — Confidential. To share this, the manager must get approval and ensure the data is encrypted. Additionally, the external vendor must sign the Vendor Security Agreement, and their access must be time-limited and logged.
